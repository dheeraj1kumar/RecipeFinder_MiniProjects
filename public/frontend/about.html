Who are our Allstars?

Social media influencers, registered dietitians, grillmasters, and more seasoned home cooks make up our enthusiastic squad of 100+ brand ambassadors. This diverse, food-loving crew spans the U.S. geographically and represents many different cultures, ethnicities, and family makeups. Since 2011, the Allstars have created tens of thousands of original recipes, photos, and reviews plus shared their cooking expertise via flat and video content on our website, social media, plus more marketing channels.

Home cooks are our heroes!

Allrecipes is the world's largest community-driven food site and our Allstars give a voice to the more than 60 million home cooks who visit each month. From meal planning to smart grocery shopping to seasonal cooking trends, this group of power users is regularly tapped for their subject matter expertise. In short, the Allstars make all our home cooking adventures more successful.

"I was a side hustle blogger and a home cook with dreams of something bigger. I'm now a full-time content creator, seasoned recipe developer, and two-time published cookbook author. I wouldn't be where I am today without the experience I have gained creating recipes for Allrecipes and being part of the Allstar program."

— ALLSTAR RAMONA CRUZ-PETERS

Are you the next Allstar?
